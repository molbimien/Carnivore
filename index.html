Going Carnivore

Supercharge your health with the carnivore diet

Simply put, when you adhere to the carnivore diet, you eat like a carnivore. That is all foodstuffs that originate from the animal kingdom.

Hype, hoax or healer?

Already in the early 1970s, dr Robert Atkins proposed the idea that by restricting carbohydrate intake, the body will burn more fat stored in the body. Unfortunately, the low carb diet program that later became known as the Atkins diet was quickly considered quackery by most of the medical community. Therefore, it is only recently that a new understanding has started exploring how different foodstuffs affect our body.

For several decades, nutritionists and medical professionals around the world were confident that the consumption of to much fat, and especially saturated fat found in animal products, was a major risk factor for metabolic diseases in general and cardiovascular diseases in particular.

However, more and more data suggests that the hypothesis was wrong all along and today the high consumption of carbohydrates is being acknowledged as a more potent risk factor.
